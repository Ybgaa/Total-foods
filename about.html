About Total Foods

Fast-growing · Est. 2022 · Food Distribution Across Egypt

Company Profile

Who We Are

Total Foods is a newly established and fast-growing company in the field of food distribution. It was established in 2022 with the aim of filling part of the gap in the market and distributing high-quality food products in a timely and efficient manner to wholesale, retail customers and key customers.


Since our founding, we have grown rapidly — reaching over 15,000 customers across Cairo, Qalyubia, and Sharkia — with ambitious plans to expand further across Egypt.

Est. 2022 · Egypt

Our Partners

Trusted Manufacturers

Total Foods cooperates with many major local companies in the field of food production and distribution.

Silo Foods Company

One of the National Service Projects Authority companies affiliated to the Ministry of Defense, a pioneer in the field of food industries.

Egypt Foods Company

A leading company in the field of snacks production such as Tiger, Excellence, protein snacks and a wide range of chips and sweets products.

Helw Al Sham Company

One of the largest companies in the field of food industries, sweets and juices supplies.

Alex Foods Company

One of the most important companies in the production, packaging, distribution and export of food — including edible oils, rice and pasta.

Camara Happy Sweet Company

One of the largest companies operating in food industries, with a wide range of up to 1,500 products including chocolate, cake, children's sweets, and biscuits.

Seco Salt Company

One of the most important companies working in the field of salt and tuna. Total Foods distributes its tuna products in the Egyptian market.

Al-Lahimy Company

One of the modern, fast-growing companies working in the field of processed and frozen meat products.
